**Design Philosophy per Archetype:**

- **Druggie:** Explosive damage windows while High, constant risk management around Withdrawal. Legendaries let you stay high forever or turn High into AOE triggers. The tension between "push for more High" and "survive the crash" creates interesting decisions every turn.

- **Influencer:** Weak turns 1-3, then snowballs hard. Early cards focus on surviving and gaining Followers. Once thresholds are hit (10 for auto-Block, 20 for extra draw), the deck becomes a machine. Legendaries accelerate the snowball or weaponize your Follower count directly.

- **Chef:** The sustain tank. Flavor stacks heal every turn, so even 10 Flavor is 10 HP/turn. Offensive cards scale off Flavor for occasional burst. The max of 20 Flavor (25 with Signature Dish) caps the healing to prevent total invincibility. Legendaries double the healing rate or convert Flavor into massive single-turn plays.

- **Jew:** The attrition DoT build. Guilt is permanent and self-compounding with the right cards. The 10+ Guilt threshold (20% less damage) and 20+ threshold (skip attacks) mean surviving gets easier the longer the fight goes. Legendaries make Guilt snowball exponentially — Generational Trauma + Covenant means Guilt feeds itself and hits double, ending fights without ever attacking directly.
