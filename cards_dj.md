
# BRIAN SIMULATOR — DJ ARCHETYPE (200 Cards)

**Keyword: Combo** — Counter starts at 0 each turn. Each card played increases Combo by 1. Many DJ cards scale with current Combo count.

**Secondary Resource: Crowd Energy** — Some cards build or spend Crowd Energy, a persistent counter that grows during the encounter.
