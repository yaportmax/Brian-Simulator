

# BRIAN SIMULATOR — JEW ARCHETYPE (200 Cards)

**Keyword: Guilt** — Stacks applied to enemy. At end of each enemy turn, they take damage equal to Guilt stacks. Guilt does NOT decay. At 10+: enemy deals 20% less damage. At 20+: enemy skips every other attack.
