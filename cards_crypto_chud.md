
# ARCHETYPE: CRYPTO CHUD (200 Cards)

**Keyword: Cash** — Combat-only currency. Earn from cards, spend on powerful effects. 20% of leftover Cash converts to Money (meta-resource) after combat.

**Junk cards** (added by some Crypto cards as downside):
- Gas Fee (0): Unplayable, sits in hand. If discarded, lose 2 HP.
- Rug Pull (0): Unplayable junk. Exhaust at end of turn, deal 3 damage to Brian.
- Worthless JPEG (0): Unplayable, clogs hand. Can be exhausted for 1 Cash.
