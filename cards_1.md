## Balance & Design Notes

**Athlete** has 18 Common, 18 Uncommon, 10 Rare, 4 Legendary. The core loop is stacking Strength with cheap buff cards (Quick Flex, Warm Up, Creatine Load) then converting it via multiplier attacks like Combo Drill (deals 3 damage per Strength point — at 8 Strength that's 24 damage for 1 energy). Titan Mode and One Rep Max provide Legendary payoffs for high-Strength builds. Body Slam creates a secondary Block-into-damage strategy.

**Gamer** has 18 Common, 18 Uncommon, 10 Rare, 4 Legendary. The core tension is "apply Poison and survive." Early turns feel weak, but cards like Venomous Code (double Poison) and Corrupted Save File (triple Poison) create explosive scaling. Hacker Mode converts stacked Poison into burst damage. Defensive tools like Rubber Banding and Gamer Chair help stall. Plague Inc. is the ultimate build-around: +2 to all future Poison application permanently warps the game.

**Hipster** has 18 Common, 18 Uncommon, 10 Rare, 4 Legendary. The 5-Record cap creates meaningful decisions — you can't take everything, so you must curate. A typical endgame board of 5 Records (e.g., Magnum Opus + Golden Record + 3 others) triggers massive end-of-turn value without playing any cards. Full Volume and Remix let you spike trigger counts. Sell the Collection provides an emergency burst option (50 damage + 25 Block from a full board). The Golden Record legendary makes every other Record trigger twice, which is the engine's crown jewel.
