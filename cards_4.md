## BALANCE SUMMARY

**Neutral Cards (40):** 15 Common / 12 Uncommon / 8 Rare / 5 Legendary

**Cross-Archetype Cards (60):** 30 Uncommon / 15 Rare / 15 Legendary (4 per pair: 2 Uncommon, 1 Rare, 1 Legendary)

**Cost curve across all 100 cards:**
- 0-cost: Quick utility plays and conditional effects
- 1-cost: The bread and butter; solid value, always playable
- 2-cost: Commit-worthy cards that swing the turn
- 3-cost: Build-around bombs that demand the full energy bar and justify it

**Cross-archetype design philosophy:** Each pair's Uncommons establish the bridge (make both keywords matter together), the Rare provides a scaling payoff, and the Legendary is the "this is why I went hybrid" moment that rewards full investment in both archetypes.
