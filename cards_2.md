## Balance Summary

| Archetype | Commons | Uncommons | Rares | Legendaries | Total |
|-----------|---------|-----------|-------|-------------|-------|
| DJ | 18 | 18 | 10 | 4 | 50 |
| Crypto Chud | 18 | 18 | 10 | 4 | 50 |
| Nerd | 18 | 18 | 10 | 4 | 50 |

**Design Notes:**

- **DJ** rewards long turns. The 0-cost cards (Scratch Record, Turntable Spin, Tempo Shift, etc.) exist to chain into high-Combo finishers like Bass Drop (Combo x 5) and Grand Finale (Combo x 8). The tension is that building Combo means spending cards on setup rather than damage/block, making the archetype feast-or-famine. Eternal Rave is the chase Legendary that lets Combo carry across turns, fundamentally changing the playstyle.

- **Crypto Chud** plays an economy minigame within combat. Cash generation cards are plentiful but many come with junk card penalties. The archetype's floor is terrible (hand full of Gas Fees and Worthless JPEGs), but the ceiling is enormous (51% Attack for 25 damage, Liquidation Event dumping 40+ Cash into damage). Satoshi's Vision doubling all Cash generation is the build-around Legendary. Rug-Proof Wallet solves the junk problem entirely, making it the defensive chase card.

- **Nerd** prioritizes consistency and inevitability. At 5+ Insight, choosing draws means always having the right answer. The archetype scales slowly but unstoppably — cards like Galaxy Brain (Insight x 4 damage) become absurd at 15+ Insight. Hand-size-matters cards (Information Overload, Full Deck) reward the draw-heavy playstyle. Omniscience removes the Insight threshold entirely, and Tenured Professor generates compounding value every turn.
